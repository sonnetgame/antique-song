Entry #38


Busy morning. We ran into a lost pilgrim in the woods near Lake Astre.

He fought. I would have too.

I cut his bloom while the others stood guard. One day he’ll realize we did him a favor.

Clover caught wind of another party that’s headed out west towards the greater plateaus, a young woman and an old man.

The plan is for me to split off from the others to track them while the rest of our group patrols the south.


Not looking forward to the heat, but at least I’ll be done with this insipid rain for a while.
