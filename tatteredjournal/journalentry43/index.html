Entry # 43



Dreadful hangover today.


i needed the extra sleep, but i just found out
that the girl left earlier this morning alone

and headed into the flats for cloudwater.

who knows where she got that idea.

wish she had just left her bloom with
the old man for safekeeping.

this would be a whole lot easier.


it's fine


can't say i blame her for keeping it close
after our last encounter


though i don’t know what sounds worse right now,
sitting here all day waiting for her to return,

or following her into the flats and chancing an
interaction with that unbearable thrift store matron.

maybe it’s just my headache talking,
but i think if i had to hear any more

of that lady's grievances,
i might actually scream at her this time.

and her damn birds.


and to make matters worse,
it’s supposed to rain here today!

it hasn’t rained here in---
