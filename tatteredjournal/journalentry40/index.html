Entry # 40



Slight detour today


before i split off from the others, gerrick suggested paying a visit to his cousin.

she maintains a fire watchtower out here and is sympathetic to our cause.

i'm grateful for the safe place to rest.


we ended up chatting for a while, and i learned about her part-time work as an archivist of dreams.

she's been comparing regional accounts and trying to determine whether

there's a connective thread between what different people imagine in their sleep.

her walls are covered with colorful sketches and letters that people have sent in over the years.

fascinating stuff.


i asked if she's ever considered joining us,

as i imagine the travels would be helpful for her research.

she said it crossed her mind, but the wooded isolation of the tower is crucial to her work.

she wouldn't tell me why though.


such a different life

i wish i could stay still that long
