Entry # 38



Busy morning.


We came across a lost pilgrim in the woods near Lake Astre.

He fought back. I would have too.

The others held him down while I cut his bloom.

One day he’ll realize we did him a favor.


Clover caught wind of another party that’s headed out west towards the Greater Plateaus,

a young woman and an old man.

The plan is for me to split off on my own to track them

while the rest of our group patrols the east.


Not looking forward to the heat, but at least I’ll be done with this insipid rain for a while.
