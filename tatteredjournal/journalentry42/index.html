Entry # 42



Dreadful hangover today.


And to make matters worse, It’s become abundantly clear

that the girl has gone into the Flats for Cloudwater.

How naive.


I had hoped she would have just left her bloom with the old man for safekeeping,

but she brought it with her.

Likely out of an abundance of caution after our last encounter.

Warranted, I suppose.


I don’t know what sounds worse right now, sitting here all day waiting for her to return,

or following her into the Flats and chancing an interaction with that insolent thrift store matron.

Maybe it’s just my headache talking but I think if I had to hold a conversation

with that woman right now I might actually murder her. And her damn birds.


And to add even more to my annoyances, it’s supposed to rain here today!

It hasn’t rained here in 4 years! Just as I left the rain, it followed me here.


Clover would laugh and say that's karma.

Maybe when I get back, I'll have to murder him too.
