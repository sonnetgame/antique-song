Entry # 42



I got tipped off by a traveling merchant that the duo were headed

to the Arres station near the Yeast Flats.

I ran ahead and set up camp on the hill above.

They arrived shortly after.


I have to keep my distance but I wish I could stop down for a drink.

I’ve been to this station before and they brew their ale

much stronger than anywhere else in the area.


Reminds me of Teague’s.

One of the only things I miss about home.




Okay.


Maybe I can sneak down for ONE drink.
