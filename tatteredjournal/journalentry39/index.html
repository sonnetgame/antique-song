Entry # 39



I caught up with that duo today, but they got away. Somehow?


It happened faster than I could figure out what was going on.

I had them cornered by the boulder overpass to the north and then something... flashed.

The rocks on the cliff above us shifted.

Stones fell. Dust scattered.

They got away.


I’ve run through it a few times in my head but I still can’t quite place the whole ordeal.

The old man doesn’t bear a bloom and the girl looked as surprised as I was,

so I only have guesses as to what actually happened.

A strange clash of fate I suppose.


Whatever.

No use in getting distracted by frustration.


They can't have gotten far.
