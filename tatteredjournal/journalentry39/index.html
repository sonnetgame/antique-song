Entry # 39



I confronted that duo today,
but they got away.

somehow?


it happened faster than i could understand.

i had them cornered by the rootway on-ramp
and then something... flashed.

the rocks on the cliff above us shifted.

stones fell. dust scattered.

they fled.


i've run through it a few times in my head
but i still can’t quite figure it out.

the old man doesn’t bear a bloom
and the girl looked as surprised as i was,

so i only have guesses as to what actually happened.

a strange clash of fate i suppose.


whatever.

no use in getting distracted by frustration.


they can't have gotten far.
